Relation Catalog

Metaplasma ships with a pragmatic preposition set (v1) spanning spatial, temporal, structural, causal, and agency domains. Each relation is a Sigil (type: relation) with metadata: directionality, symmetry, inverse, arity, roles, and category.

  • Open catalog (Markdown)

  • in ⇄ contains; on ⇄ supports; at
  • under ⇄ above; inside ⇄ around; outside ⇄ surrounds
  • between (arity 3); among (member,set)
  • adjacent_to (sym); near (sym); far_from (sym)

  type: relation
  properties:

    arity: 2
